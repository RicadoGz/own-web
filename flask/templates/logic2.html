The inspiration for developing this program came from a question I heard: "If you knew you could be successful, what project would you create?" Considering the current job market, I believe that helping people find real job opportunities is a valuable endeavor (and it would also help me find a job). This program helps you locate software companies and apply for jobs directly through their websites, as I don't have much faith in LinkedIn. By using web scraping techniques on Google Maps, I've made it easier to discover potential employers. While these skills might not seem particularly fancy, I believe that the ability to break down a large project into smaller, manageable tasks is far more important than learning flashy new technologies.
